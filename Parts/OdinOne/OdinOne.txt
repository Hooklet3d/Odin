Odin One Initial

=============================================
Published under GPL v3
Organization: Hooklet3d
Developer: Steven Griffin
Date 09/2016
============================================

The Odin One is the initial release of the Odin Reprap 3d printer developed by Hooklet3d
this initial model is a Prusa varient and intended to be a 'jumping off' point for future 
iterations.

This folder will contain the files needed to print a large portion of the Odin One.
